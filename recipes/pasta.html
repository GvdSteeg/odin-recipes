Pasta

Description

Enjoy a nice bowl of pasta?

Better make sure it is prepared correctly. The secret? It is all in boiling the pasta!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of tagliatelle
  • 240 grams of bacon
  • 20 minutes of your fucking time

Steps

  1. Cut the bacon into strips. Yes, bacon STRIPS!
  2. Meanwhile, boil the pasta for a perfect amount of time. Yes, SUCK IT!
  3. Throw it in a bowl and ENJOY!
